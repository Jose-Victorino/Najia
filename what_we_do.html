What We Do

  • Industrial Rigging

    It is the process of moving heavy tonnage/sensitive machinery and equipment with the use of internationally recognized methods and special rigging tools. Prior to actual commencement rigging operations will conduct site survey, analysis, planning and validation.

  • Moving & Transferring

    Moving of machines within the area or transfer of machines to another manufacturing plant through the supervision of a team and skilled personnel.

  • Packing & Close Crating

    An activity that involves the design, fabrication and assembly of crating with packing materials for machines and equipment for shipment or warehouse storage.

  • Material Sourcing

    NAJIA MOVERS can source out, negotiate, purchase, acquire, deliver equipments, tools, supplies and materials from suppliers and manufacturers.
